CCN 2026 — Abstract #504 (Submitted)
Arousal as an Unpredictable Dimension: Evidence from THINGS/THINGSplus
Poojak Patel
Using the THINGS and THINGSplus object concept databases, we demonstrate that arousal ratings are uniquely resistant to prediction from other perceptual and semantic object properties — suggesting arousal captures a distinct, poorly-understood dimension of human object perception.
